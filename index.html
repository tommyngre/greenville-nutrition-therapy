About Me

Hi! I’m Julie and I thank you for taking the time to get to know me.

I am a Greenville transplant originally from Atlanta, GA. I am a registered dietitian, licenced dietitian in the state of SC and the founder of Greenville Nutrition Therapy. Since 2012, I have specialized in the treatment of eating disorders and adolescent health. Discovering the joy I received from helping individuals navigate their journey to freedom with food and contentment in caring for their bodies has led me here to Greenville Nutrition Therapy. I work to individualize each client’s treatment plan while collaborating with all of your community providers including your therapist, primary care providers and psychiatrist, as needed. I am committed to providing weight inclusive, culturally sensitive, evidence based treatment to help all individuals achieve freedom and full recovery from their eating disorder. I regard diversity and believe that all diversity is to be valued and honored.

I hold a bachelors of science in exercise science from the University of South Carolina and a masters of science in nutrition from Georgia State University. I started my career in an eating disorder treatment facility after completing my coordinated nutrition program. I have worked in levels of care ranging from outpatient to partial hospitalization and was the nutrition supervisor at the Eating Recovery Center of the Carolinas prior to starting my private practice.

I believe…

  • That it’s not about what you eat; it’s about how you eat
  • That eating disorder recovery is possible
  • That your body is good
  • That chocolate should be eaten every day...if you like it
  • That you can eat freely and healthfully and those are not mutually exclusive
  • That nutrition science can empower you
  • That “healthy” is holistic and much more than simply diet and exercise
  • That healthcare should be weight inclusive vs weight-normative
  • That eating and movement are meant to be enjoyed

Professional Memberships…

Insurance

Out-of-Network Servies

I am, currently, not in network with any insurance providers meaning your insurance cannot be billed directly. I can provide you with a “superbill” that you can submit for possible reimbursement for out-of-network nutrition services. Please be aware that submission of the superbill is your responsibility.

Payment

Cash, check all major credit cards, or your HSA card are accepted as payment at the time of service.

Cancellation

All appointment cancellations or changes must be made 48 hours in advance of your scheduled appointment. Any appointments canceled or changed within 48 hours of the scheduled appointment will be billed at the full rate of the scheduled appointment.
